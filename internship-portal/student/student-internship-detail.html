UI/UX Designer Intern
Innovation Tech
Job Description

Innovation Tech is seeking a motivated and creative UI/UX Design Intern to join our dynamic team. You will work closely with our design and product teams to create engaging and user-friendly interfaces for our web and mobile applications. This is a great opportunity to gain real-world experience in a fast-growing tech company.

Responsibilities
  • Assist in creating wireframes, mockups, and prototypes.
  • Conduct user research and usability testing.
  • Collaborate with developers to ensure design implementation quality.
  • Contribute to the development and maintenance of our design system.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest UI/UX trends and technologies.
Qualifications
  • Currently enrolled in a degree program related to Design, HCI, Computer Science, or a similar field.
  • Portfolio showcasing UI/UX design projects (coursework or personal projects acceptable).
  • Experience with design tools such as Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD.
  • Understanding of user-centered design principles.
  • Good communication and teamwork skills.
About Innovation Tech

Innovation Tech is a leading provider of cutting-edge software solutions. We pride ourselves on fostering a collaborative and innovative work environment. Join us to make an impact!
